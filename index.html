Get Ready for North America's Ultimate Facial Hair Championship!
The North American Competitive Beard Moustache Alliance (NACBMA) and Salty Saints Social Club & Facial Hair Society invite you to North America's ultimate charity facial hair championship! Join us up in the alpine beauty of Utah's Wasatch mountains at Snowbird Resort on June 25th for the 2022 Great American Beard & Moustache Championship, Presented by Honest Amish! We have 4 days of fun and activities lined up.
A grassroots event held in even-numbered years, and backed by 60 facial hair clubs across the United States & Canada, the GABMC gathers together international competitors sporting the finest, biggest, and best beards and moustaches from North America and beyond for charity & bragging rights.

A few details

  • Tickets, registration, and category information is available in our Categories section.
  • We'll have blocks of rooms at group rates at Snowbird suitable for everyone from couples and families to groups. With everything centralized, the events will be within strolling distance from your room.
  • Keep an eye out for events planned Thursday & Friday nights, and of course a brunch Sunday.
  • There's plenty to see and do in the area, and with so many national and state parks within a few hours drive, it's a great opportunity to make a mini-vacation of the trip and explore the wonders of America's Mountain West.

    • We would like to take a moment to thank everyone once again for their support of the 2018 GABMC in Richmond. We had over 340 competitors representing 39 states (including Alaska!), 3 Canadian provinces, the U.K., Germany, and Japan, and raised an astounding $25,220 for The Humane Society of the United States & the Pink Ink Fund, a record for any single-day facial hair competition anywhere!
